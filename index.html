What is matchday?

Matchday is a sports facility located on New B.E.L road, Bangalore. We currently have two 5-a-side pitches and cricket nets for training.

Why matchday?

Why matchday?

We use turf that's Fifa 2-star certified. We've also taken care to ensure that factors like pitch dimensions, line markings and lighting are in accordance with FA standards. We are probably the most conveniently located pitch in North Bangalore. Also, we have parking!
